Brytpunkt

audiovisual fixed media work commissioned by KAC i Rosa Huset

Brytpunkt treats the crossing of worlds, their coalescence and separation, and finding something where nothing was.

Using purely synthesised computer-generated sounds and visuals, Brytpunkt (eng. breaking point) explores the superimposition and sepration of different layers of reality. Point clouds shaped by a Lorenz system, depth of field simulations and images of faces let different kinds of breaking points unfold fragmented in space. The music is in large part performed on the scarda digital musical instrument.

premiered 2019-11-22 at Rosa Huset in Skövde
