cyber|glow

public interactive installation
by re|thread
Erik Natanael Gustafsson, Thomas Durieux, Jonathan Ramirez Mendoza and Benoit Baudry

cyber|glow is a light installation, generated in real time based on the interactions of the visitors. It reveals the invisible and live software traces that operate an interactive game.

Photos: edling.agency

Credits

cyber|glow was designed by the rethread.art software art collective.

Erik Natanael Gustafsson, Thomas Durieux, Jonathan Ramirez Mendoza and Benoit Baudry were supported by the KTH Royal Institute of Technology, the CASTOR center for software research, the NAVET hub for science and art and the Wallenberg Autonomous Systems, Software and AI Program (WASP).

The installation was developed in collaboration with the Nobel Week of Lights.
